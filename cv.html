bio

Maria Orciuoli (b. 1989, Avellino) is an artist working across performance, installation, and system design. Her practice integrates live action and custom software to expose how rule-based systems shape movement, timing, and legibility. With a background in social sciences and electronic arts, she holds degrees from Luiss Guido Carli University, SRH Berlin, INSEEC Paris, and the University of Arts Linz. She has held residencies at the Digital Nature Group in Tsukuba, Japan, and at Das Weisse Haus in Vienna. Her work has been presented at Ars Electronica, Krupa Art Foundation, and Cairotronica, among others.

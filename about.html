Dear reader, I consider it a privilege to be in the midst of your day, and thank you for devoting time and attention to the contents shared on this site.

I'm an artist and cultural publicist working in the fields of music, video, digital culture and technology.

I run KO-HUM, a communications agency for artists and cultural producers working in music and performing arts.

Another part of what I spend time working on is an alternative language I can use to think and talk about our socities. This process often results in the creation of performance, video and sound works.

I graduated in Economics in Rome, and completed a dual masters degree in International Management Studies while living in Berlin and Paris. I'm currently MA student at the Interface Cultures department of the University of Art & Design in Linz.

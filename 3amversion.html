Blending text, image, and photography, 'Past Post-Global Network - The 3 AM Version' reports the results of a personal experiment on how technology informs my private life. The project is a kaleidoscope of interfaced situations, adventurous and idiosyncratic, merging online search results, news headlines, automated messages, spam, digital archives, and views from multiple screens.

In my 30s, I don't know what's important. Inevitably, something grasps my imagination and I go with it...

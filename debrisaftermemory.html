This short evokes a poetic of the elements that make visible the mechanics of reality and the symbolism in the built environment. The filming technique exists in the ambiguous space between documentary and cinematic realism.


Debris resulting from subtractive processes become a tool of storytelling. Metal, wood, earth, water, and fire embody memory as a process in which fragments of earth, labor, and emotion consolidate into a singularly charged moment. Perhaps, it's violence and how all things are shaped at will or turn to dust. All of these and none of them - and what's the difference anyway, what's the core of matter; what's the truth?

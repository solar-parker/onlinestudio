update 17.06.2022

I came back revisiting this project. The images below are microscopic views of the raindrops collected in April. The patterns should be deposits / dust particles. I was unable to spot microorganisms inside (I kept the raindrops in the fridge). Somewhere inside of every raindrop is a tiny impurty - a touch of salt, a grain of clay). In my understanding, these particles are relevant to the raindrop's existence because water vapor can't condense into droplets on its own. The entire process is described in the book by Prupacher and Klett: Microphysics of Clouds and Precipitation.
